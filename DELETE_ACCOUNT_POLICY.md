# Account Deletion Policy

## How to Delete Your Order Kro Account

### In-App Account Deletion

1. **Open the Order Kro app**
2. **Go to Settings** (Menu → Settings)
3. **Scroll to "Account Settings"**
4. **Tap "Delete Account"**
5. **Review the deletion notice**
6. **Confirm deletion** by entering your phone number
7. **Your account will be permanently deleted**

---

## What Happens When You Delete Your Account

- ✓ All personal information will be removed
- ✓ Delivery addresses will be deleted
- ✓ Order history will be cleared
- ✓ Payment methods will be removed
- ✓ All saved preferences will be deleted
- ✓ Your account cannot be recovered after deletion

---

## Important Notes

- **Immediate Effect:** Account deletion is effective immediately
- **Non-Recoverable:** Once deleted, your account cannot be recovered
- **New Account:** You can create a new account with the same phone number after 30 days
- **Support:** For issues with account deletion, please contact our support team

---

## Data Retention

- After account deletion, your data is retained for compliance purposes for 90 days
- After 90 days, all personally identifiable information is permanently deleted
- We do not share deleted account data with third parties
